Eagles

The grip of the bald eagle is actually 10 times stronger than a human. Despite their much smaller bodyweight, the eye of an eagle is about the same size as a human eye. Eagles can carry up to four times their own body weight, making it one of the strongest birds in the world .

Lions

While lions are generally social animals, prides generally consist of 80% females. For this reason, only about one in eight male lions survive to adulthood. In 18th century England, the price of admission to the Tower Menagerie (the pre-cursor to the London Zoo) was three pence, or a cat or dog to be fed to the lions! The lion is considered one of the “African Big Five.”
